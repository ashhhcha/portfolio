Who am I?

(as a designer)

I am a designer who loves prototyping and using my skills to support communities through designed experiences. Ethical design is important to me, and I actively work to make a positive impact through design.

My Design Pillars.

(the principles that guide my work)

Usability is key.

Patricia Moore's quote, "Stop designing for disability and start designing for usability," greatly influenced my design approach. I aim to create designs that are usable to everyone and promote an inclusive experience for all users.

Always a student.

I seek opportunities to learn and explore various design areas as they're interconnected uniquely. The continuous learning of design propels me to explore refreshing methods and approaches to my design.

Further with community.

Sharing knowledge and collaborating with one another will further the project. I value building a space and community with respect for all voices.
