Who Am I?

(as a designer)

I am a designer who loves prototyping and using my skills to support communities through designed experiences. Ethical design is important to me, and I actively work to make a positive impact through design.

My Design Pillars

Usability is key.

Patricia Moore's quote, "Stop designing for disability and start designing for usability," greatly influenced my design approach. I aim to create designs that are accessible to everyone and promote an inclusive experience for all users.

Learning is a collaborative process.

There will be ideas and perspectives that others can offer an alternative and further the project's success. I value keeping an open mind to others' voices and input.
