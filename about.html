Hello there! I'm Ashley (she/her),
a UX/UI Designer

Thanks for making it this far into my portfolio website! I'm currently pursuing UX/UI design as a career. I became interested in this field after years of scouring lists of potential career paths. Although I was interested in STEM, I wanted to find a job that also allowed for creative freedom. There was difficulty finding a job that had a perfect mix of technology and art, until I stumbled upon UX/UI design. This field presents a unique balance that allows me to design with a purpose and goal. Especially after working with Figma, I developed a passion for making elements animate, and creating a flow between frames.

My Design Pillars

Usability is key.

The Patricia Moore's quote, "Stop designing for disability and start designing for usability," greatly influenced my design approach. I aim to create designs that are accessible to everyone and promote a positive and inclusive experience for all users.

Learning is a collaborative process.

There will be ideas and perspectives that others can offer an alternative and further the project's success. I value keeping an open mind to others' voices and input.
